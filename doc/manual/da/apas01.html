A.1. Forudsætninger

If you encounter bugs during your install, please refer to Afsnit 5.4.7, “Indsendelse af installationsrapporter” for instructions on how to report them. If you have questions which cannot be answered by this document, please direct them to the debian-boot mailing list (debian-boot@lists.debian.org) or ask on IRC (#debian-boot on the OFTC network).
